The Path to Enlightenment

Your 5-Day Exclusive Itinerary

A carefully curated timeline designed to maximize your spiritual experience while minimizing fatigue.

Day 1: Arrival & Yamunotri

Depart from Dehradun. Fly to Kharsali and proceed to Yamunotri for your first Darshan. Enjoy a tranquil evening at your luxury resort.

Day 2: The Sacred Gangotri

Morning flight to Harsil. A scenic drive brings you to the Gangotri temple. Experience the majestic evening Aarti along the Bhagirathi river.

Day 3: Majestic Kedarnath

Fly directly to Sersi/Phata, then to Kedarnath helipad. Experience the profound energy of Lord Shiva's abode with VIP Darshan.

Day 4: Blissful Badrinath

Journey to Badrinath. Participate in the sacred Maha Abhishek Pooja and explore the enchanting Mana Village.

Day 5: Return to Dehradun

Concluding rituals and a scenic return flight to Dehradun, carrying divine blessings and unforgettable memories.

Detailed Itinerary

A closer look at your 5-day luxurious spiritual journey.

Day 1: The Origin - Yamunotri

  • 07:00 AM: Departure from Sahastradhara Helipad, Dehradun.
  • 07:45 AM: Arrival at Kharsali Helipad. Welcome drinks.
  • 09:00 AM: Palki/Pony ride to Yamunotri Temple for Darshan.
  • 01:00 PM: Return to Kharsali, check-in to luxury resort, and lunch.

Day 2: The Sacred Flow - Gangotri

  • 08:00 AM: Flight from Kharsali to Harsil Helipad.
  • 09:00 AM: VIP transport to Gangotri Temple.
  • 11:00 AM: Exclusive VIP Darshan at Gangotri.
  • 02:00 PM: Return to Harsil, nature walk, and premium resort stay.

Day 3: The Mighty - Kedarnath

  • 07:30 AM: Flight from Harsil to Sersi/Phata.
  • 08:30 AM: Shuttle flight to Kedarnath Helipad.
  • 09:30 AM: Guided walk to the temple, VIP Darshan of Lord Shiva.
  • 02:00 PM: Return to Sersi, evening at leisure.

Day 4: The Supreme - Badrinath

  • 08:00 AM: Flight to Badrinath Helipad.
  • 09:00 AM: Check-in at Sarovar Portico or equivalent.
  • 11:00 AM: VIP Darshan at Badrinath Temple.
  • 03:00 PM: Local sightseeing including Mana Village (last village of India).

Day 5: The Return

  • 04:00 AM: Optional Maha Abhishek Pooja at Badrinath.
  • 09:00 AM: Breakfast and flight back to Dehradun.
  • 10:30 AM: Arrival at Dehradun. Tour concludes with divine memories.
