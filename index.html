Information Center

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the yatra?
The typical Chardham Helicopter Yatra from Bangalore takes 5 to 6 days depending on weather conditions and your preference.
Is VIP darshan included?
Yes, VIP Darshan assistance is included at all four shrines to ensure a quick and comfortable experience.
Are senior citizens allowed?
Absolutely. Our service is specially designed to cater to senior citizens, minimizing physical exertion through helicopter transfers and ground support.
What is included in the package?
The package includes helicopter transfers, VIP darshan, luxury accommodation, all meals, local transport, and a dedicated coordinator.
What should we carry?
We provide a detailed list upon booking, but generally: warm clothes, comfortable walking shoes, personal medicines, and valid ID proof.
